The Story

Born in Casablanca, raised on the hypnotic rhythms of Gnawa music and the pulsing beats of electronic dance music. Mohamed ALAOUI bridges two worlds — the ancient mystical sounds of Morocco and the cutting-edge energy of modern house and techno.

From the rooftops of Marrakech to the clubs of Ibiza, Mohamed brings a unique sonic journey that transports audiences across continents and centuries.
