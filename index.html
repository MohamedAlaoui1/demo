The Artist

Born in Casablanca, raised on the hypnotic rhythms of Gnawa music and the pulsating beats of electronic dance music. Mohamed ALAOUI crafts a unique sonic experience that bridges continents and generations.

With over 10 years behind the decks, performances at major festivals across Morocco, Europe, and beyond, Mohamed brings an unmistakable energy that transforms every venue into a journey through sound.
